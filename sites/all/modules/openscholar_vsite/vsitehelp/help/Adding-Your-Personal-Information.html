The Users section of the Control Panel allows you to manage your account and personal information. You can add or edit your personal information by clicking on the Personal Information tab.

Adding your first and last name is highly recommend for disambiguation and it allows other users on the site to find you more easily.

Entering your professional title, such as "Professor of Economics", is also recommended. You are allowed to enter as many titles as you want.

Upload and Crop your photo - You can upload a photo and crop it to the default size and shape by grabbing the cropping tool handles and adjusting to your preference. You'll a preview of the cropped photo to the left of the cropping area. You'll want to make sure the original image you upload is at least the size of the default image (180px by 140px).

Once you've added your name, title and photo you're on your way to building your site.
